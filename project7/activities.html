Guide to New York City: Activities

Now that you are full from eating, you can go back and do some activities that NYC has to offer. Here are some places you can visit for a wide range of activities:

Broadway Shows

There are always shows happening on Broadway that you can go and visit. They are pricey; however it is well worth for an extremely well live performance! Personally, my favorite is The Lion King, as it is one of the classics that Broadway is known for!

The Bronx Zoo

The Bronx Zoo is one of the most popular attractions you can visit and see over 6000 different animals! It does require a ticket, however they are affordable. There are also time slots to see trained professionals feed sea lions and penguins.

New York Public Library

if you are a fan of books, there are thousands of books, ready for you to check out at any of the NYPL chains. On a hot or cold day, staying indoors at a library is a great way to spend your time.

The Rink at Rockefeller Center

If you can ice skate, or want to learn and fall over and over in the process, this is a great place to visit with some friends and relax on an ice skating rink for a bit. There are also a ton of great restaurants in the area if you get hungry after ice skating!

Watch a Mets Game at Citifield

If you are interested in baseball, the New York Mets very often play at CitiField. You can come here and watch a game, however be advised as usual, prices for everything at sport stadiums are jacked up extremely high!
